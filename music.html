Music

My favorite albums

Aquemini by Outkast

This is exactly the kind of album I grew up listening to. My two of my brothers are 10 years older than us so from a young age I've always been really into hip-hop music. I can listen to this record front to back without skipping a single song

Bandana by Freddie Gibbs and Madlib

When Freddie Gibbs and one of the most legendary hip hop producers linked up in 2011 for their first track my mind was blown. It was the best duo I never asked for. Freddie is notorious for his gritty and real street raps and Madlib is famous for his soulful beats coupled with your favorite oldies sampled. Bandana is their second album and in my opinion it was the best piece of music to come out in 2019
