Goals

My goals in life are simple, I want to take the best care I can of everybody who has watched out for me my whole life. My parents and my older brothers have always been there for me financially and emotionally. I want to be able to take care of them one day like they have for me in my life. I have a young brother as well, and I want to make sure I can do the most for him I possibly can.

Owning a home

Owning a home has also always been a dream of mine. My mom used to always take me on drives when I was little down Christmas Tree Lane even when it wasn't Christmas and I always thought it would be cool to live on Van Ness. I know it's not easy but I hope to make it there one day.

Starting a family

Another goal of mine is to start a family with a woman I love. My dad has made it extremely apparent that he wants more grandchildren also lol. So maybe one day after I am married and I have got my career on track I want to start a family.
