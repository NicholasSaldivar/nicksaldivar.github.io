Pets

Pets are a big part of my life as well. I currently have two dogs. One is a Maltese and Yorkie mix, and the other is a pure maltese. Their names are Ella and Posey. I have always had dogs growing up, in fact one of my dogs just passed away about a year ago. Her name was Yaya and she lived to about 17 years old I believe. My mom got her for us, I remember coming home one day and seeing a puppy under my moms desk and my mom introduced us to each other. We had dogs before Yaya, but they were pitbulls and they were always too aggressive to play with us as kids. Dogs are a big part of my life now and they always will be. I want to buy another dog soon, I am looking at border collies, and bordoodles in case I can't stand the dander.
