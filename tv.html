TV Shows

These are my favorite TV shows of all time

The Sopranos

I recently just started rewatching this show again, I watched it once when I was younger with my mother so it kind of brings fond memories of the past and I watched it again as an adult. Anyway this show is a staight up classic. It has some of the best dialogue in any show I have seen and now when I'm tuning into something else I always feel like something is left to be desired. It's not like any other organized crime related form of media it has you really rooting for Tony and his family.

Futurama

This show is so clever, this is probably my feel good and background noise show. I used to always have it playing in the backround while I'm doing some other task. The characters are original and all have their own little hilarious quirks. Just a great and underappreciated I feel show
