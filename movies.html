Movies

Below are some of my favorite movies of all time

Eternal Sunshine of The Spotless Mind

This movie does to me exactly what I want out of movies, it makes me feel something. This movie is about this Couple named Joel and Clementine and after they go through a break up they contact this Company called "Lacuna" and the company allows them to erase every memory they have with that person, however they always still end up together in the end. I won't spoil the rest

American History X

This movie follows a man with a troubled past named Derek Vinyard. When Derek was younger a random act of violence was committed on his firefighter father while on duty- Derek goes on to blame every race outside of his own and joins a skinhead gang and later ends up in prison. It's in prison where he really starts to reform, his gang betrays him and he befriends and African American while working in the prison. When he gets out of prison finally he tries to go straight and erase all of the hate in his life but he now has a new problem on his hand, his little brother was following in his footsteps. This movie I feel is still relevant today and I like how it showed that you can always progress in your darkest times and turn your life around.
